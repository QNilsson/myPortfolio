Welcome!

The object of this game is to guess the number with as few tries as possible. To play, click the 'Get Number' button, then typ in your answer and press 'Submit'. If you are wrong, keep tyipng in a new number and pressing 'Submit' until you get it right. To play again, refresh the page and press 'Get Number' again. Good Luck!
